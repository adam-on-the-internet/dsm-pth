Procedural Rules of the City of Des Moines

This is general information about the Procedural Rules of the City of Des Moines. This is not official and should not be taken as any sort of legal advice. If you need an official copy of the rules, please contact the City Clerk, as the rules are not otherwise available (at the time of this page's creation on 09/15/21).

The rules are adopted and amended through Resolution action, not Ordinances. Some additional meeting guidelines are in Section 2-70 of the City Code available here: MUNICODE

  • Rules Version 16 - Consent Requests Forbidden (Current)

    As amended on June 28, 2021

    Rule Documents:

    Original PDF Web Page Transcription

    Amendment Information:

    Full Agenda Resolution for Agenda Item Roll Call

    These rules were amended at a Regular Council Meeting on June 28, 2021 in agenda item 1-I.

    Rule 37. Action on Consent Agenda was amended as follows:

    • If members of the public wish to request an item be pulled from the consent agenda, they must send a request to the City Clerk by 3pm the day of the meeting (via email, mail, or other writing).
    • No such oral requests may be made at the meeting.

    These rules were put in place at the start of the meeting (around 5pm) and took effective immediately. That meant that at 5pm, council passed a requirement that anyone wishing to make a request must have made the request before 3pm, over 2 hours earlier.

    The vote passed unanimously and no public comment was permitted.
